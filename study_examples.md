# SmartFlash: Study Note Examples & AI Generated Flashcards

This document contains sample study paragraphs and the flashcard outputs generated by the local spaCy NLP engine for each of our five parsing rules.

---

## 📝 Rule 1: Definition Matching (Copula Verb Structures)
* **How it works**: Looks for sentences with root verbs like `"is"` or `"are"` and constructs a `"What is/are..."` question.

### Example Note:
> *"Photosynthesis is the chemical process plants use to convert sunlight into energy. Mitochondria are the primary organelles responsible for cellular respiration."*

### Generated Flashcards:
* **Card 1**:
  * **Question**: `What is Photosynthesis?`
  * **Answer**: `The chemical process plants use to convert sunlight into energy`
  * **Difficulty**: `Easy`
* **Card 2**:
  * **Question**: `What are Mitochondria?`
  * **Answer**: `The primary organelles responsible for cellular respiration`
  * **Difficulty**: `Easy`

---

## 👤 Rule 2: Biographical Facts (Person Entities)
* **How it works**: Detects a `PERSON` named entity at the start of a sentence acting as the subject and constructs a `"Who..."` question.

### Example Note:
> *"Albert Einstein developed the theory of relativity. Alan Turing invented the Turing machine in 1936."*

### Generated Flashcards:
* **Card 1**:
  * **Question**: `Who developed the theory of relativity?`
  * **Answer**: `Albert Einstein`
  * **Difficulty**: `Medium`
* **Card 2**:
  * **Question**: `Who invented the Turing machine in 1936?`
  * **Answer**: `Alan Turing`
  * **Difficulty**: `Medium`

---

## 📍 Rule 3: Geographical / Location Facts
* **How it works**: Matches sentences containing prepositions of place (`in`, `at`, `on`, `inside`) and formulates a `"Where..."` question.

### Example Note:
> *"Photosynthesis occurs in chloroplasts. The Declaration of Independence was signed in Philadelphia."*

### Generated Flashcards:
* **Card 1**:
  * **Question**: `Where does photosynthesis occur?`
  * **Answer**: `In chloroplasts`
  * **Difficulty**: `Easy`
* **Card 2**:
  * **Question**: `Where was the Declaration of Independence signed?`
  * **Answer**: `In Philadelphia`
  * **Difficulty**: `Medium`

---

## 📅 Rule 4: Chronological / Time Facts
* **How it works**: Matches sentences containing a `DATE` or `TIME` entity preceded by a temporal preposition and formulates a `"When..."` question.

### Example Note:
> *"The university was founded in 1895. World War II ended in 1945."*

### Generated Flashcards:
* **Card 1**:
  * **Question**: `When was the university founded?`
  * **Answer**: `In 1895`
  * **Difficulty**: `Easy`
* **Card 2**:
  * **Question**: `When did World War II end?`
  * **Answer**: `In 1945`
  * **Difficulty**: `Easy`

---

## 🕳️ Rule 5: Fallback Cloze Deletion (Fill-in-the-Blank)
* **How it works**: For descriptive sentences that don't match Rules 1-4, it automatically extracts a high-priority noun or direct object, hides it with `______`, and makes a fill-in-the-blank card.

### Example Note:
> *"Plants use sunlight to perform cellular synthesis. Cells extract chemical energy from glucose."*

### Generated Flashcards:
* **Card 1**:
  * **Question**: `Complete the statement: Plants use ______ to perform cellular synthesis.`
  * **Answer**: `Sunlight`
  * **Difficulty**: `Medium`
* **Card 2**:
  * **Question**: `Complete the statement: Cells extract chemical energy from ______.`
  * **Answer**: `Glucose`
  * **Difficulty**: `Easy`
